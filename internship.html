Computer scientists are professionals who study and develop computing technologies. They work in various fields such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and software development. Their innovations have transformed industries, enabling advancements in automation, data processing, and communication.

Some of the most influential computer scientists include Alan Turing, who laid the foundation for modern computing, and Grace Hopper, who contributed significantly to programming languages. Their contributions continue to shape the future of technology.
